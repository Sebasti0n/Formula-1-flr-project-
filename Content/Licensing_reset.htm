Resetting 's activation status

If you have a problem with the licences on your computer, Elecosoft's Technical Support department may ask you to reset the licences on the computer. Resetting the licences removes all Elecosoft licences from your computer, which means that you will not be able to use any Elecosoft software on the computer until the licences have been restored. You should only do this under the supervision of Elecosoft's Technical Support department.

To remove all Elecosoft licences from this computer:

  1. Click the File tab, click Activation, select in the Products group then click Reset. The Licence Reset dialog appears.
  2. Elecosoft's Technical Support department will email you a reset key. When you receive the reset key, copy it to the Windows® Clipboard, then click Paste to paste it into the field on this dialog.
  3. Click OK. All Elecosoft licences are removed from the computer.

You can then restore the licences on the computer, under the supervision of Elecosoft's Technical Support department.
