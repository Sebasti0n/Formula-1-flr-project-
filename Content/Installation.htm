Installation

Step 1: Prepare Wirebox

 

The default conduit size is 3/4 in (21 mm). 1 in (27 mm) conduit is acceptable if needed. Based on fittings and conduit size, prepare the wirebox.

• For top or bottom entry: Manually remove the conduit plug.

• For rear entry: Drill with 1-1/8 in (29 mm) step bit to prepare wirebox for fittings
